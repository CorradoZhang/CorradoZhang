## About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Galway, working on adaptive intelligence for decision-making in uncertain environments.

My research sits at the intersection of reinforcement learning and robotics, with a focus on building autonomous systems that can learn, adapt, and act robustly in complex settings.

## Current Focus

- Designing learning algorithms for autonomous agents
- Exploring robust and adaptive decision-making methods
- Building research prototypes across ML, optimization, and robotics

## Background

- PhD in Computer Science, University of Galway, 2025-2029 (expected)
- MSc in Artificial Intelligence, University of Galway, 2024-2025
- BEng in Computer Science and BSc in Mathematics, Heilongjiang University of Science and Technology, 2020-2024
